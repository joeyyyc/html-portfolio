Best Animes According to Joey

Top 3 Animes of All Time

One Piece

The plot is really interesting and the main characters are wholesome.
My favourite character is Zoro as he is very badass in a cool way.

Haikyuu

I love watching the volleyball matches and the character development behind my favourite characters, Hinata and Kageyama.
The team spirit is hyped and I like to see how they never give up and persevere on.

Demon Slayer

The animation is the best out of all the animes I have watched.
The comedy is spot-on and the action is really gooooooood.
